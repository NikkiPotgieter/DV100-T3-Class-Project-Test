The Alocasia zebrina is difficult to find and tricky to grow since it is known for being picky about its growing conditions. Native to the rainforests of the Philippines, this plant requires similar conditions—moist, well-draining soil, dappled sunlight, warm temperatures, humidity, and soil enriched with compost or fertilizer.
